Stories

1. GET /stories

View all stories

This allows you to see a summary (ID and title) of all stories in the db

2. GET /stories/:id

Get a story with by an ID

This allows you to get one story (ID, title, and content), but using its ID to find it.

3. POST /stories

Create a story

This allows you to write a story. Title is required.

4. PUT /stories/:id

Modify a story

This allows you to find a story by its ID and change its title or its content

There should be a text box and submit button for each: title and content. Or maybe just one submit button for all. Or something else. I need to stop thinking about it for now.

5. DELETE /stories/:id

Delete a story

This allows delete a story by its ID.
